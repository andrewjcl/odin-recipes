Easy Scones

Recipe from https://www.taste.com.au/recipes/basic-scones/

Fill your home with the smell of freshly baked scones.
This classic favourite is sure to be a crowd pleaser.

Ingredients
  • plain flour
  • 3 cups self-raising flour
  • 80g butter
  • 1.5 cups milk
  • jam
  • cream
Instructions
  1. Preheat over to 200 deg.
  2. Rub butter into the flour.
  3. Add milk.
  4. Gently knead dough.
  5. Lightly dust baking tray and lay out dough.
  6. Bake for 20 minutes or until golden brown.
  7. Serve warm with jam and cream.
